Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Tap any locked feature or the crown icon in the app to open the upgrade screen. You can choose between a yearly plan ($9.99/year with a 3-day free trial) or a monthly plan ($2.99/month).

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → MoneyTree → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access will remain active until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID. Simply sign in with the same Apple ID and your Pro access will be restored automatically. You can also tap "Restore Purchases" on the upgrade screen.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your data — saved scenarios, calculator inputs — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers. If you delete the app, your data is removed.

How accurate are the calculations?

MoneyTree uses standard financial formulas with monthly compounding for growth calculations and standard amortization for loans. Results are for illustrative purposes and should not replace professional financial advice.
